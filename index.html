About Us

Sci Tales is a platform for sharing stories about the benefits of open science.

We believe that open science has the potential to democratize knowledge and empower everyone to participate in the scientific process. We publish stories from researchers, practitioners, and other stakeholders who are working to advance open science. We also provide resources to help people learn more about open science and how to implement it in their own work.
